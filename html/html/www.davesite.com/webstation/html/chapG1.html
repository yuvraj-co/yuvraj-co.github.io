Generating Revenue with your Site

How can I generate revenue with my site?

If your host allows you to have commercial banners and advertisements on your site, there are a variety of companies that will pay you for placing banners or buttons or sometimes even search boxes on your site. Generally, there are four ways to generate revenue:

  • Cost per Ad displayed: usually referred to as CPM (or cost per thousand shows), this was a once common form of advertising on the Internet. For smaller sites, it seems to have died off, although a few sites still pay it. This type of advertising means that you get paid a certain set amount for every time someone loads your page.
  • Cost per Click on Ad: usually referred to as a CPC (cost per click), this is probably one of the most common forms of advertising on the Internet. Usually payout is from 1 cent per click to 25 cents per click. This means you will get money any time someone clicks a banner on your site.
  • Cost per Action: usually called a CPA, this is a form of payment for when someone does something, such as fills out a form, orders a service, or orders a product. Commissions sometimes fall into this category.
  • Commission: Payment when the someone buys something.

What kind of traffic (number of visits) do I have to have to be profitable?

It really varies. If your site only gets five hundred visitors per month, you may make less than a dime. Then again, you could make a few bucks. It all depends on circumstances. If your site gets thousands of visitors per month, you will probably make quite a few bucks.

Getting started

First you need to check if your host allows banners. Next, you check out the options (there are many others, I've just included a select few I've found reliable.) Bookmark this page to use as a reference. When you sign up for one of the banner programs, they will give you HTML code to place on your page(s). But once you have the HTML code up, you don't have to do anything else (except update your site once in a while) to earn money.

I've come across a relatively new company recently that allows you to design and sell t-shirts, mugs, mousepads, and more through their site at no cost to you. You design the images, upload them and customize them until you're satisfied, and then place a link on your site to your personal "store" where they can be purchased online. You don't have to take the orders or ship the products personally, it is all done through this company. (They just send you checks for your sales!) You set the price above the cost, and the difference is pure profit! (E.g. say the mousepad base price is $10.99. If you sell it for $11.99, you'll make $1 per mousepad, at $12.99, you'll make $2 per mousepad, etc...)
